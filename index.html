COVID-19 GENERAL HEALTH INFORMATION

Protect Yourself. Everyone Should:

  • Wash your hands often
  • Avoid close contact
  • Wear a mask around mouth and nose when around others
  • Clean and disinfect often
  • Monitor your personal health ans symptoms

Symptoms:

  • Fever/Chills
  • Shortness of breath
  • Coughing
  • Fatigue
  • Sore throat
  • Congestion
  • Nausea
source: https://www.cdc.gov/coronavirus/2019-ncov/prevent-getting-sick/prevention.html
